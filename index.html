The Story

✦✦✦✦✦

Doing things scared

I didn't come to Customer Success to play it safe. I came to figure things out — even when the path wasn't clear, even when the tools were new, even when the answer wasn't obvious yet.

In my first year at Fullbay, I earned a Trailblazer Award and two High Performing Awards — not because everything came easily, but because I kept digging. If there was a wall, I looked for a door. If there wasn't one, I built it.

That same instinct is what led me to AI automation. I watched my team spend time on tasks that should take minutes. I started asking: what if we didn't have to? So I learned Zapier. I learned to prompt AI. I built five working tools from scratch — independently, outside of work hours — because the tools existed and I wasn't going to be afraid of them.

"There has to be a better way" isn't a complaint. It's a compass.

I manage accounts ranging from SMB to enterprise — up to 50+ users — and I bring the same energy to every one: proactive, human, and always moving toward value.

The Grimoire

Five Tools Built From Scratch

Five AI-powered automations built independently — outside any employer — to show how I think about Customer Success at the intersection of technology and human relationships. Each is live, deployable, and grounded in real CSM workflows.

01 · Zapier + OpenAI + Gmail

Churn Risk Detection Alert System

Why I built it → Because by the time you notice churn, you're already behind.

When a customer row is updated in Google Sheets, AI analyzes their health score, usage patterns, support tickets, and renewal timing — then sends a CSM-ready intervention plan to the inbox. Risk level, root cause, three action items, and a pre-written outreach message. Automatically.

Google SheetsZapierOpenAI GPT-4.1Gmail

Built an AI-driven churn detection system that identifies at-risk accounts and generates real-time intervention strategies, reducing reactive support workflows.

02 · Zapier + OpenAI + Gmail

Welcome Email Generator

Why I built it → Generic onboarding emails don't build relationships. Personalized ones do.

The moment a new customer is added, AI reads their company, industry, and plan tier — and writes a fully personalized 30-day onboarding welcome email tailored to their business. Delivered to the assigned CSM before they've even opened their laptop.

Google SheetsZapierOpenAI GPT-4.1Gmail

Designed an AI onboarding automation that generates personalized 30-day plans instantly upon customer creation, accelerating time-to-value.

03 · Schedule + Google Sheets + OpenAI + Gmail

Weekly Portfolio Health Digest

Why I built it → Monday mornings shouldn't start with scrambling.

Every Monday, this automation pulls the entire account portfolio, runs it through AI analysis, and delivers a prioritized weekly action plan — risk levels, recommended actions per account, and an executive summary of overall portfolio health.

Schedule TriggerGoogle SheetsZapierOpenAI GPT-4o

Built an automated weekly portfolio health system that surfaces at-risk accounts and generates prioritized action plans every Monday morning.

04 · Zapier + OpenAI + Gmail

Meeting Prep Generator

Why I built it → The best customer calls start before you dial in.

Drop in account data and receive a complete call prep document — three personalized talking points, top risks to address, expansion opportunities, five suggested questions, and a full executive account summary. Ready in seconds.

Google SheetsZapierOpenAI GPT-4.1Gmail

Automated meeting preparation using AI to generate strategic call prep docs, enabling more focused and effective customer conversations.

05 · Zapier + OpenAI + Gmail

NPS Response Classifier

Why I built it → Customer feedback is only valuable if you actually act on it.

When a customer NPS response is logged, AI classifies sentiment, tags the category, assigns urgency, identifies the key theme, recommends a CSM action, and flags churn risk — all before a human even reads it.

Google SheetsZapierOpenAI GPT-4oGmail

Developed a Voice of Customer classification system using AI to automatically tag NPS responses and surface actionable retention insights.
